Tips to Help Reduce Waste

  • Include amounts of items you want to purchase in you're shopping list to avoid over purchasing.
  • Only buy what you know you will use.
  • Look for products that use less packaging.
  • Donate unused or unwanted items.

Mission Statement

We created Reduce, Reuse, Recycle in order to help people realize the importance of recycling as well as how they can save money. We hope that our website is a valuable resource to those who need ideas for how they can live better.
