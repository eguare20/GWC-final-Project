Climate Change

Earth

The Earth is the 5th largest planet in the solar system. It is the only planet known to harbour life.

What is Climate Change

Climate Change is the periodic change of the Earth's climate.

How Humans Impact Climate Change

  • We release Carbon Dioxide when we exhale, but also when we do activities such as deforestation and burning fossil fuels.
  • Methane is produced during the decomposition of waste in landfills, agriculture, and domestic livestock waste management.
  • Nitrous oxide is produced from soil cultivation practices. This includes fertilizers, fossil fuel combustion, etc.

Mission Statement
