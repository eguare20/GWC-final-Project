General Saving Help

Tips to help

  • Set up a budget
  • Do not spend your full credit if you do not pay it off completely each month
  • Use cash only -- this helps you to stick to your budget

Apps to help

Walla.by: helps consumers think more intelligently about credit card usage and how to make credit work for you.

Honey: a free browser extension that automatically finds and applies coupon codes at checkout on more than 30,000 shopping sites

Digit: essentially makes money-saving decisions on your behalf by looking at your current income and expenditures and then calculating what you can afford to save
