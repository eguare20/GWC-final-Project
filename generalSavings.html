General Saving Help

Tips to help

  • Set up a budget
  • Do not spend your full credit if you do not pay it off completely each month
  • Use cash only -- this helps you to stick to your budget
  • Look for a cheaper cell phone plan. Consider moving to a month-to-month plan, especially if you are single.

Apps to help

Honey: a free browser extension that automatically finds and applies coupon codes at checkout on more than 30,000 shopping sites

Digit: essentially makes money-saving decisions on your behalf by looking at your current income and expenditures and then calculating what you can afford to save

Links to help

  • The Balance: 100 Ways to Start Saving Money Today
  • The Simple Dollar: How to Save Money: 100 Great Tips to Get You Started

Mission Statement

We created Reduce, Reuse, Recycle in order to help people realize the importance of recycling as well as how they can save money. We hope that our website is a valuable resource to those who need ideas for how they can live better.
