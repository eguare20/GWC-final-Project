Reuse

Tips to help reuse

  • Buy used items. They can often be just as good as new.
  • Donate item you don't use anymore
  • Buy reusable items rather than disposable items
  • Save jars and containers to reuse
  • Compost

Apps to help

eBay: app to buy and sell used items

Goodwill Locator: find local Goodwill donation centers

LetGo: buy and sell used items
