Cars

Tips to help save money with your car

  • Before buying a car consider the cars mileage and what type of gas the car requires
  • Keep cars longer instead of trading them in
  • Think twice before buying warranty from dealerships becuase they often over charge
  • Keep up with car maintence
  • Check air filters monthly -- a dirty filter with shorten engine life
  • Pump your own gas
  • Don't "top off the tank" -- this coul lead to losing some gas
  • Don't idle -- you waste a quart of gas every 15 minutes of idling
  • Look into reciving a "Good Student" discount on insurance for teen drivers
  • Avoid tickets for speeding or moving violations

Apps to help

GasBuddy -- helps to find the lowest gas prices in your area

Waze -- gives you alerts with fastest route so that you don't waste as much gas with stoping and starting

GasGuru -- helps to show where the cheapest gas is. For example, it can tell you if there is cheaper gass by your work or home
